\section*{Quiz: Prediction and Loss in Binary Classification}

\subsection*{Model Setup and Engineering Context}

Consider a binary classification model
\[
f(u(x)\mid w),
\]
where:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $x \in \mathbb{R}^d$ is the feature vector,
    \item $w \in \mathbb{R}^d$ is the weight vector,
    \item $u(x) = w^\top x$,
    \item $f(\cdot)$ is an activation function.
\end{itemize}

The task is post-earthquake structural rating of a damaged building with two labels:
\[
\textit{Safe}, \quad \textit{Unsafe}.
\]
